The Atacama Fashion Week is a unique event held in Chile’s Atacama Desert, aiming to shed light on the environmental issues caused by discarded clothing waste. It transforms this challenging landscape into a fashion runway where models showcase upcycled designs made from clothing salvaged from waste piles. The event brings attention to the pressing need for sustainable fashion, blending style with environmental awareness in a dramatic, real-world setting.
